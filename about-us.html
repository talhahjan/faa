Fazal Al Abbasi Construction Company (FAC) was established a decade ago with the concept of providing Electro-mechanical products and integrated solutions related to operations and maintenance. We have developed a prestigious clientele over the past years and achieved significant milestones in the industry. Our team consists of qualified and well experienced technical and engineering personnel who are capable of providing necessary technical guidance for most appropriate economic solutions without compromising quality.

  • Construction (Design, Excavation, Structure Building, Finishing, Interior Designing)
  • Electrical (Design, Installation, Maintenance and Operations of Electrical Control Panels)
  • Electronic (Design, Installation, Maintenance and Operations of PLCs / Control systems)
  • IT, CCTV & Networking (Design, Installation and Maintenance of IT infrastructure)

Construction

We have a wide range of Schneider Electric, ABB, CHinT and LS Electrical components. We design and manufacture MCC and other related Electrical Control Panels. We also provide Uninterrupted Power Systems (UPS) solutions by EATON for Power-sensitive equipment.

Electrical Products & Solutions

We have a wide range of Schneider Electric, ABB, CHinT and LS Electrical components. We design and manufacture MCC and other related Electrical Control Panels. We also provide Uninterrupted Power Systems (UPS) solutions by EATON for Power-sensitive equipment.

Networks & CCTV Camera systems

We specialize in design & implementation of IT infrastructure, Networking Solutions, IP PBX Phones as well as CCTV systems.

Thermal Mapping, Monitoring & Automation Solutions

Monitoring your factory equipment, consumption of utilities and automating it will add value to your business. We have exclusive solutions by our partners like Q3Logics Inc. USA and Blulog EU. for Data-logging & Thermal mapping.

Annual Maintenance Contracts

We specialize in design & implementation of IT infrastructure, Networking Solutions, IP PBX Phones as well as CCTV systems.

HVAC Equipment Supply & Installation

We are proud partners with leading brand names of the HVAC Industry. Over past several years, we have carried out infrastructure design, installation and operations for several projects across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia
