About Us

Who are we & what do we do ?

Fazal Al Abbasi Construction Company (FAC) was established a decade ago, with the concept of providing Electro-mechanical products and integrated solutions related to operations and maintenance. We have developed a prestigious clientele over the past years and achieved significant milestones in the industry. Our team consists of qualified and well experienced technical and engineering personnel who are capable of providing necessary technical guidance for most appropriate economic solutions without compromising quality.
